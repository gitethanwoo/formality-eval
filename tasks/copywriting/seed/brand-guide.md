# Lumina Brand Guide

## Voice & Tone
- Modern, warm, approachable
- Tech-savvy but not jargony
- Emphasize the "it just works" experience
- Target audience: remote workers, 25-40, value productivity and wellness

## Key Messages
- Light that adapts to your life
- Designed for how you actually work
- No more eye strain, no more manual adjustments

## Brand Colors (for reference)
- Primary: Warm amber (#F5A623)
- Secondary: Deep navy (#1A2B4A)
- Accent: Soft white (#F8F9FA)

## Competitors
- BenQ ScreenBar ($109) — monitor-mounted, no smart features
- Dyson Lightcycle ($599) — premium, over-engineered for most users
- Xiaomi Mi LED ($39) — budget, basic smart features
